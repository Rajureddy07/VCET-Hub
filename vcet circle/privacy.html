Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025

Welcome to VCET Hub. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and services. VCET Hub is a mini-project developed by students of VCET to help learners access study materials, notes, question papers, and other academic resources.

Interpretation & Definitions

Definitions

  • VCET Hub (“We”, “Us”, “Our”) – refers to the VCET Hub project and its developers.
  • Service – refers to the VCET Hub website and all features on it.
  • You – the user accessing the website.
  • Device – any device such as mobile, laptop, or tablet used to access VCET Hub.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

VCET Hub does NOT collect sensitive personal information. We may collect only the following minimal information:

  • Email address (only if you contact us)
  • Name (if you provide it manually)
  • Comments you submit in modules

Usage Data

We may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Time spent on pages
  • Pages you visited

Cookies

VCET Hub uses cookies only for:

  • Improving user experience
  • Saving theme or preference settings

You can disable cookies in your browser settings anytime.

How We Use Your Data

Your data may be used for:

  • Improving the website
  • Fixing bugs and performance issues
  • Showing previews, downloads, and module content
  • Moderating comments or feedback

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data with third parties.

Data Retention

We retain only the necessary information required to operate the Service. Comments or uploads may remain unless you request deletion.

Security of Your Data

We use standard security measures to protect your data. However, no website can guarantee 100% protection.

Children’s Privacy

VCET Hub is intended for college students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Links to Other Sites

VCET Hub may contain reference links to other educational websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary. Any updates will be posted on this page.
